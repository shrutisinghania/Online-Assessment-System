Before the Online Exam: Prepare

1. Read and understand the test guidelines.
2. Know the test format.
3. Test yourself. If your instructor makes a practice exam available to you, take it!
4. Study the class materials!
5. Plan your time.
6. Determine when you will take the test.
7. Gather all that you’ll need to take the test.
8. Take a deep breath! Once you’re logged in, take a moment to relax and get focused.



During the Online Exam: Focus

1. Keep an eye on the clock.
2. Print and save copies of the test questions, as well as your answers.
3. Don’t leave the test page!
4. Technical problems? Don’t panic. But do contact your instructor .
5. Check your work before you submit it.
6. Click submit.



After the Online Exam: Review

1. Assess your own progress.
2. Check your grade.
3. Ask yourself how you can improve on the next exam.
